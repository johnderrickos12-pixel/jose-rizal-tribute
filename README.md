# Jose Rizal Tribute

A simple web-based tribute to Dr. Jose Rizal, the national hero of the Philippines.

## Overview

This project provides an overview of Jose Rizal's life, his significant contributions, and his enduring legacy.

## Features

- **Early Life:** Information about his birth, family, and early education.
- **Journey to Europe:** Details of his travels and studies abroad.
- **Literary Works:** Highlights of his seminal novels, "Noli Me TÃ¡ngere" and "El filibusterismo."
- **Execution and Legacy:** The events leading to his martyrdom and his lasting impact on Philippine history.
